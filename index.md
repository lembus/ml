# Preface {.unnumbered}

This book is a comprehensive curriculum covering the foundations and modern practice of Machine Learning. It is organized into eight core modules, ranging from mathematical foundations to the deployment of generative AI agents.

## Structure of the Book

1. **Mathematical & Statistical Foundations**: Probability, Estimation, and Causal Inference.
2. **Core ML Paradigms**: Supervised and Unsupervised Learning techniques.
3. **Neural Networks**: From Perceptrons to Transformers and GNNs.
4. **Optimization**: Theory, Loss Functions, and Tuning.
5. **Data-Centric ML**: Feature Engineering and Feature Stores.
6. **Evaluation**: Metrics and Robustness.
7. **MLOps**: Deployment, Monitoring, and Scalability.
8. **Domain Applications**: NLP, Computer Vision, RL, and GenAI.
